# Mangarr Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Mangarr contributors, maintainers, and community members commit to providing a
welcoming, professional, and harassment-free environment for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, ability, or personal
circumstance.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and documented outcomes.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction when impact differs from
  intent.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's private information.
- Help keep discussions relevant and accessible to participants with different
  levels of experience.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized content, personal
attacks, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, release
channels, and other spaces where someone represents the Mangarr project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject
contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when this
policy is violated. Enforcement decisions will consider severity, repetition,
impact, and the safety of affected people.

For ordinary moderation, contact the maintainer through the repository. For a
sensitive report that should not be public, use GitHub's private reporting link
in `SECURITY.md` and begin the report title with `Conduct:`. Reports will be
handled as privately as the platform permits.

This policy is informed by the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.
